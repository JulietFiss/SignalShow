Cassegrain Aperture

The Cassegrain aperture is a thick anulus that is 1 where r is greater than the inner radius but less than the outer radius.

Parameters:

  • Amplitude, A0
  • X Center, x0
  • Y Center, y0
  • Inner Radius, r0
  • Outer Radius, r1
