Circular Aperture

The circular aperture is a circle that is 1 inside of its radius and 0 outside of its radius. Another name for the circular aperture is the cylinder function.

Parameters:

  • Amplitude, A0
  • X Center, x0
  • Y Center, y0
  • Radius, r0
