Salt and Pepper Noise

Salt and pepper noise, or randomly occurring black and white pixels, is commonly found on images. The amount of noise is controlled by f, the noise fraction: f/2 pixels will be black, f/2 pixels will be white, and the remaining pixels will be 0.

Parameters:

  • center (mean).
  • f, the noise fraction.
  • seed, the seed used for the pseudorandom number generator.
