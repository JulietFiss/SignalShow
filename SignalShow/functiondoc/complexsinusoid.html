Complex Sinusoid

The complex sinusoid is a complex exponential with a linear phase. The real part of the complex exponential is a cosine function, and the odd part is a sine function. The complex sinusoid has a constant magnitude.

To edit the complex sinusoid, edit the parameters of either the real or imaginary part. The parameters of the other part will automatically update.

Parameters:

  • Center, x0*
*the center parameter has been added to SignalShow for convenience

Support: infinite

Area: zero for non-zero frequencies. If the frequency is zero, the sinusoid is equivalent to the constant function, and the area is infinite.

Symmetry: even (real part), odd (imaginary part) or neither, depending on initial phase.
