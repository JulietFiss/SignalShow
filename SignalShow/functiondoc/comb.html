Comb

COMB[x] is infinite train of evenly-spaced deltas.

The comb may be scaled, which changes the spacing between the deltas:

Parameters:

  • Amplitude, A0
  • Center, x0
  • Width, b

Support: infinite

Area: infinite (Dirac deltas)

Symmetry: even
