Gaussian Noise

The shape of the normal distribution is a Gaussian function. The mean value is the most likely amplitude to occur. The probability that an amplitude will differ from the mean progressively decreases as the value moves away from the mean.

Parameters:

  • standard deviation, σ
  • mean, μ or
  • seed, the seed used for the pseudorandom number generator.

mean: μ

variance: σ2
