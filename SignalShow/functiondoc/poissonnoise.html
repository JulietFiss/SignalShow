Poisson Noise

The Poisson probability law is an approximation to the binomial law for large numbers of rarely occurring events, i.e., n >> 1 and p ~= 0. The Poisson distribution has one parameter, λ, which controls both the mean and the variance. As λ becomes large, the Poisson distribution more closely resembles the Binomial distribution.

Parameters:

  • λ mean and variance
  • seed, the seed used for the pseudorandom number generator.

Mean: λ

Variance: λ
