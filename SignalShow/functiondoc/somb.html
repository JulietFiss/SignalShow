Besinc (Sombrero function)

The besinc function is analogous to a sinc function, but instead of being constructed with a sine in the numerator, a J1 Bessel function is used.

The besinc function is also given the name "sombrero function" because of its resemblance to the Mexican hat. It is often abbreviated SOMB[x]

Because the denominator is zero at the origin, the SOMB[0] is defined to be 1.

The besinc function may be shifted and scaled.

Parameters:

  • Amplitude, A0
  • Center, x0
  • Width, b

Support: infinite

Symmetry: even
