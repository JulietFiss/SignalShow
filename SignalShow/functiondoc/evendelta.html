Symmetric Delta Pair

Unit delta functions located at +1 and -1

The symmetric delta pair may be scaled:

Parameters:

  • Amplitude, A0
  • Center, x0
  • Width, b

Support: 2 (scaled by width)

Area: 2 (Dirac deltas)

Symmetry: even
