Cauchy-Lorentz Noise

If U and V are two independent normally distributed random variables with mean 0 and variance 1, then the ratio U/V has the standard Cauchy distribution. The shape of the Cauchy-Lorentz distribution is that of the Lorentzian function

Parameters:

  • x0, width parameter
  • γ, center (median and mode)
  • seed, the seed used for the pseudorandom number generator.

Mean: undefined

Variance: undefined
