Double Slit

A symmetric pair of RECT functions with the same width and amplitude. When creating the double slit, the endpoints of the rectangles are ignored.

Parameters:

  • Amplitude, A0
  • Center, x0
  • Width (distance between centers of slits), b
  • Slit Width, w

Support: b + w

Area: 2*b*A0

Symmetry: even
