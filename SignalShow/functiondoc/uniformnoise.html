Uniform Noise

In the uniform distribution, any amplitude within the allowed range of amplitudes is equally likely to occur. The shape of the probability density function of a uniform distribution is a rectangle.

Parameters:

  • width, b
  • mean, μ
  • seed, the seed used for the pseudorandom number generator.

mean: μ

variance: width2/12
